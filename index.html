xtw

xtw.app is a Mac application that gives you a number of taskwarrior tasks remaining and tasks overdue in your menu bar. It doesn't occupy space in your Dock, doesn't have any configuration, and doesn't take up much memory.

I find it useful as a mental indicator of 'papers on my desk' and an incentive to turn the mean red into black by wiping out some tasks.

It's open source and BSD licensed on GitHub and written by Tom MacWright.
